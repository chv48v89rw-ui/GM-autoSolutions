Privacy Policy

GM autoSolutions respects your privacy and works to protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when you register, submit enquiries, or contact a dealership. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and message details.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to support vehicle enquiries, user account management, and to help connect buyers with dealerships.

3. Liability and Data Responsibility

GM autoSolutions is not liable for how dealerships use or handle buyer information after an enquiry is sent. Any personal data shared with a dealership is controlled by that dealership and subject to its own privacy practices.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with third parties only when necessary to provide our services or where required by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to or deletion of your account information. For any privacy concerns, contact us at info@gmautosolutions.ke.
