🤖 Welcome to the AI Car Assistant

Get personalized vehicle recommendations from the cars currently listed on our website. The AI searches our database to help you find the best match for your needs.

📌 How to Use the AI

Be as specific as possible. Include details such as:

  • Your budget
  • Preferred make or model
  • Body type (SUV, Sedan, Hatchback, Pickup, etc.)
  • Fuel type (Petrol, Diesel, Hybrid, Electric)
  • Transmission (Automatic or Manual)
  • Year range
  • Number of seats
  • Features you want (Sunroof, Leather Seats, Apple CarPlay, 4WD, etc.)

Ask one clear question at a time for the most accurate recommendations.

You can also ask the AI to compare vehicles, explain features, or help you choose between different options available on the site.

💡 Example Questions

"Show me SUVs under KSh 2 million."
"Recommend a reliable first car for a university student."
"Which automatic Toyota has the lowest mileage?"
"Compare the Mazda CX-5 and Toyota Harrier."
"Find me a fuel-efficient sedan under KSh 1.5 million."

📊 Powered by Our Listings

The AI recommends vehicles from our website's current inventory. If a vehicle isn't listed, the AI won't recommend it.

💬 Daily Usage Limit

  • Every user receives 10 AI messages per day.
  • Use detailed questions instead of several short ones to get the most value from your daily limit.
  • Your message count resets every day.

💡 Pro Tip

The more details you provide in a single message, the better and more personalized the AI's recommendations will be. 🚗
